## Recommended Starting Point

**Start with these two items first:**

1. **Landing Page** - Biggest blocker for sharing. People need to understand what they're signing up for.

2. **Mobile Responsiveness** - Many users will access on phones. Draft especially needs work.

These unlock the ability to share the app link publicly and have users successfully onboard.
